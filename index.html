Portfolio Website for a Composer

In 2023, I built a showcase for a friend of mine, who is a composer. He had made a rough design with wix, which I then converted into a "proper" website. As he wanted to be able to add, modify and delete content at any point in the future, I decided on a CMS approach. Additionally, we settled for a headless CMS both liked the decoupling of the content from the design, which was pretty set in stone anyway.

In the end, I decided on Contentful as a CMS. I built the front end using Gatsby, since I liked the integration it provided with Contentful. I had also recently learned of chakra-ui, and the "utility-based" components and well thought out theming system seemed like a good fit for this project.
